CareSync Privacy Policy

What we collect, how we use it, where it lives, and your choices.

CareSync is a student project. It’s for demonstration and evaluation only. Not a medical device. By using CareSync, you agree to this Policy and the Terms of Service.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account: name, email, hashed password; for doctors, license/credentials and profile details.
  • Interactions: appointments, call metadata, chats/messages, prescriptions, reminder settings.
  • AI history: prompts you submit, generated responses, and references to images used in multimodal queries.
  • Uploads (doctors): license/ID/profile photo stored on the server (instance/uploads by default).
  • Technical: device/browser info, pages/events, and error logs to keep things running.

3. How We Use Data

  • Provide features: accounts, appointments, video calls, chat, prescriptions, reminders.
  • Support and improve the service, troubleshoot issues, and keep the system reliable.
  • Send transactional emails and reminders you enable.
  • Protect users and the service (abuse prevention, security monitoring).

4. AI Processing

  • AI prompts and referenced images may be sent to Google’s Gemini API to generate replies.
  • Don’t submit real-world PHI or confidential medical records. This is an academic demo.
  • Your prompts and responses are stored with your account to show history.

5. Calls & Files

  • We keep minimal metadata needed to connect and manage video calls and quality safeguards.
  • Files shared during calls may be stored temporarily and may be watermarked; they are scheduled for deletion after the room closes or within a reasonable period.

6. Storage & Location

Data is stored in our server-side database via SQLAlchemy. Development uses a local SQLite database (e.g., medbot.db); other deployments may use a managed DB. Uploads live under a private server directory (instance/uploads) unless configured otherwise.

7. Security

  • Passwords are stored using one-way hashing (e.g., Werkzeug utilities).
  • We apply transport-level protections and session safeguards appropriate for this project.
  • No method is perfect. Report suspected issues using the contact below.

8. Sharing

  • Service providers that help run features (email, AI processing) under appropriate agreements.
  • Disclosures required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • We do not sell personal data.

9. Your Choices & Deletion

  • Update basic profile info in your account.
  • Delete your account from the Account page to remove your profile and associated records after a short safety window for queued operations.

10. Changes

We may update this Policy. The “Last updated” date shows the effective version. Using CareSync after changes means you accept the updated Policy.
